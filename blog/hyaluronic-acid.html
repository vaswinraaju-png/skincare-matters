What Is Hyaluronic Acid?

Hyaluronic acid is a naturally occurring molecule in our skin that helps retain moisture and keep skin hydrated.

Benefits of Hyaluronic Acid

  • Deep hydration
  • Plumper-looking skin
  • Reduced dryness lines

Best Way to Use Hyaluronic Acid

Apply on damp skin and seal with a moisturizer to prevent moisture loss.

Myths & Misconceptions

Hyaluronic acid is not an exfoliating acid and does not thin the skin.

FAQs

Is it safe for oily skin? Yes.

Can I use it daily? Absolutely.
